IUH

Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City (English: Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City) is an application-oriented and practice-oriented university, directly under the Ministry of Industry and Trade, specializing in industrial economics training. and industrial engineering, was established on December 24, 2004.
On September 24, 2018, the university obtained the AUN-QA accreditation certificate of the ASEAN University Network organization, certifying that the university's qualifications are valid for use in Southeast Asian countries.

FIT - IUH

The Faculty of Information Technology (IT) was formed in 1996. After a long journey of continuous efforts to improve and develop, the Faculty of Information Technology is gradually asserting its prestige with the motto "Training students to be solid. theory, good practice and master new technology”.
Vision: To become a leading prestigious unit in training and research in computer and information technology, integrating with the region and the world
Mission: To provide the society with human resources with professional qualifications and skills in the field of computers and information technology to meet the requirements for the socio-economic development of the Industry and Trade industry and the country. effectively in the context of international integration.
